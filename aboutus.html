About Us

Wish Fic Fest is a community-run celebration of NCT WISH fanfiction. Our goal is simple: bring readers and writers together through open prompts, supportive deadlines, and a cozy space to create.

Our Mission

  • Celebrate all pairings and story genres in the NCT WISH universe.
  • Make it easy to join : whether you’re a first-time writer or a seasoned author.
  • Encourage collaboration through public prompts and gentle guidance.

How It Works

  1. Prompters submit ideas via the form; prompts are made public.
  2. Writers sign up to self-prompt or claim one public prompt (first come, first served).
  3. Fics are posted on AO3 under the WishFicFest collection starting Sep 30.

What We Value

  • Kindness & credit. Be respectful, cite inspirations, and credit beta/co-authors.
  • Creative freedom. Any rating and trope is welcome with proper tags & warnings.
  • Accessibility. You don’t need Twitter; email is fine.
